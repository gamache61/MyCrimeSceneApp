# MyCrimeSceneApp

A browser-based detective mystery game: **"The Midnight Murder at Blackwood Manor"**.

## What is this?

You play as a detective called to Blackwood Manor, where Lord Edmund Blackwood has been found dead in his locked study at midnight. Three suspects remain on the premises — and one of them is the killer. Your job is to examine the crime scene, collect evidence, review suspect profiles, and make an accusation before the trail goes cold.

## How to Play

1. **Open `index.html`** in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).  
   No server or installation needed — just double-click the file.

2. **Click "BEGIN INVESTIGATION"** on the title screen.

3. **Examine the Crime Scene** — click on highlighted objects in the room to collect evidence:
   - 🔪 Bloody Knife
   - 🧶 Torn Piece of Cloth
   - 🍷 Wine Glass (with lipstick)
   - 👣 Muddy Footprint
   - 📜 Crumpled Note
   - 🕛 Broken Pocket Clock

4. **Review the Evidence Board** — see all clues you've collected and what they reveal.

5. **Study the Suspects** — read each suspect's motive, alibi, and suspicious detail:
   - Lady Evelyn Cross
   - Dr. Victor Harlow
   - Mr. Thomas Briggs

6. **Make Your Accusation** — once you've collected at least **4 pieces of evidence**, the accusation panel unlocks. Choose your suspect and submit.

7. **Win or lose** — if you're right, the full story is revealed. If you're wrong, keep investigating!

You can restart at any time using the **↺ Restart** button in the top-right corner.

## Technical Notes

- Pure vanilla JavaScript, no frameworks or build step required.
- All styling uses CSS custom properties (variables) for easy theming.
- Fonts loaded from Google Fonts: [Cinzel](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cinzel) and [Crimson Text](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Crimson+Text).
- Responsive layout works on both desktop and mobile browsers.
