THE GRAND CROWSNEST CASTLE MURDER

A Murder Mystery Investigation

It is the night of the Winter Solstice Ball at the ancient Crowsnest Castle. The grand ballroom is filled with the sound of dancing and conversation.

At precisely 11:47 PM, the lights suddenly go out. A single, piercing scream cuts through the darkness.

By the time the authorities arrive, the body has already been removed and the crime scene processed. All that remains are the official photographs taken at the scene.

The heavy oak doors were locked from the inside. No one entered or left the room.

You are Detective Jim Smart, known to many as "Bull", the tough and relentless investigator assigned to the case. The killer is still somewhere within the castle walls.

Your task: Study the crime scene photographs carefully. Examine every clue in the rooms of Crowsnest Castle. Piece together what really happened before the trail goes cold.

The crime scene was in the ballroom.
